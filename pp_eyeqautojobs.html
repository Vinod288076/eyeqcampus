EyeQ Campus

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Introduction

EyeQ Campus is a mobile application designed for Schools, Colleges, Universities and other Educational Institutions. The application allows Students and Parents to securely access academic information provided by their respective Campus.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the EyeQ Campus application.

Who Can Use EyeQ Campus?

  • Students
  • Parents
  • Educational Institutions

Accounts are created and managed only by the respective School or Campus.

Account Information

  • Usernames and Passwords are provided by the Campus.
  • Students cannot create their own accounts.
  • Parents cannot register directly.
  • All account management is performed by the Campus Administration.

Features Available in the App

  • Student Profile
  • Attendance
  • Daily Homework
  • Exam Results
  • Fee Details
  • Time Table
  • Notice Board
  • School Calendar
  • Exam Date Sheet
  • Leave Application
  • Other information provided by the Campus

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal information directly from Students or Parents.

All information displayed inside the application is entered and managed by the respective Campus through its administration system.

EyeQ Campus simply displays the information that has been provided by the educational institution.

How Information Is Used

The information is used only for educational purposes such as:

  • Student Profile
  • Attendance Records
  • Homework
  • Exam Results
  • Fee Information
  • Leave Applications
  • School Notifications
  • Academic Calendar

Data Sharing

We never sell your data.

We never share your data with advertisers.

We never use your data for marketing purposes.

Student information remains under the control of the respective Campus.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or misuse.

Account Deletion

The application does not provide an account deletion option.


Student accounts are created, disabled, or deleted only by the respective School or Campus Administration.

If you wish to disable or remove your account, please contact your School or Campus directly.

Children's Privacy

EyeQ Campus is intended for use by Students and Parents under the authorization of their educational institution.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
